# CDAEIA Submission Guide

Follow these steps to successfully claim your tax credit.

## Step 1: Internal Audit
- Review your corporate structure against the NAICS requirements.
- Identify the project(s) that meet the "Significant AI Integration" criteria.
- Confirm you have at least 6 eligible employees.

## Step 2: Documentation Preparation
- Use the `Project_Description_Template.md` to document the AI logic.
- Prepare payroll extracts showing full-time status and time allocation for the eligible staff.
- Ensure your financial statements clearly show that over 75% of revenue is IT-related.

## Step 3: Application to Investissement Québec (IQ)
Requests are made *after* the end of your fiscal year.
1. **Access the Portal**: Log in to the Investissement Québec online portal (Formulaires coming soon).
2. **Submit Corporate Attestation**: Provide the corporate details to prove company eligibility.
3. **Submit Employee Attestations**: Provide the detailed records for each eligible employee.
4. **Pay the Fees**: Investissement Québec charges a fee for the analysis based on their current pricing grid.

## Step 4: Claiming the Credit
Once you received the **Attestations** from IQ:
1. Attach the certificates to your T2 and CO-17 tax returns.
2. File the claim with Revenu Québec.
3. The credit is usually processed within the standard tax assessment timelines.
