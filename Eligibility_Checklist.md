# CDAEIA Eligibility Checklist

Use this checklist to verify if your company and projects align with the Investissement Qu√©bec requirements.

## 1. Corporate Requirements
- [ ] **NAICS Compliance**: Does at least 50% of your gross revenue come from specific NAICS codes (541514, 541515, 513211, 513212, 51821)?
- [ ] **Sector Revenue**: Does at least 75% of gross revenue come from the IT sector?
- [ ] **Team Size**: Do you maintain a minimum of **6 eligible full-time employees**?
- [ ] **Location**: Are the activities performed in an establishment located in **Quebec**?

## 2. Employee Eligibility
- [ ] **Full-Time Status**: Is the employee full-time?
- [ ] **Time Allocation**: Does the employee spend at least **75% of their time** on eligible activities?
- [ ] **Direct Support**: Are they undertaking, supervising, or directly supporting work related to AI-integrated business activities?

## 3. Activity Eligibility (AI Integration)
- [ ] **Significant AI Integration**: Does the project integrate AI functionalities in a "significant manner"?
- [ ] **Core Business Link**: Is the activity linked to info systems development, infrastructure, or security services?
- [ ] **Non-Excluded**: Is the project *not* merely a transactional website, marketing system, or routine maintenance?

## 4. Documentation Readiness
- [ ] **Fiscal Year End**: Have you completed your fiscal year? (Requests are sent post-FY).
- [ ] **Payroll Records**: Are accurate salary and time-tracking records available for each employee?
- [ ] **Project Logic**: Do you have a project description that highlights the AI "Significance"?
